Our Strategy & Philosophy

Our main focus is on sustainable, ethical and long-term investing. We care about both financial returns and the impact of capital on the world.

Time Horizon

We divide our approach into long-term and medium-term buckets:

  • Long-term: multi-year holdings in quality companies or ETFs.
  • Medium-term: trades driven by macro or valuation shifts.

Risk Management

  • Position sizing based on conviction and volatility
  • Diversification across sectors and regions
  • Clear invalidation levels for trades

Sustainability Focus

We prefer businesses that align with long-term environmental and social goals, though we may still analyse and comment on wider markets for educational reasons.

This strategy describes how we personally think about markets. It is not a recommendation that you should adopt the same approach. Your circumstances, risk tolerance and goals are unique to you.

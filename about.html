About Green Tree Capital

Green Tree Capital is a small, independent project run by two friends who are passionate about sustainable, ethical and long-term investing.

We track our own portfolios publicly, share our thinking, and document our wins and mistakes so others can learn alongside us.

What We Are

  • A personal investing journal
  • An educational resource about markets and sustainability
  • A community of people learning about finance together

What We Are Not

  • We do not manage money for others
  • We do not provide personalised financial advice
  • We do not guarantee any returns
We are not financial advisors. Nothing on this site, in our Discord, or in any of our content should be taken as financial, investment, legal or tax advice. Always do your own research and consider speaking to a licensed professional.
